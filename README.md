## ğŸ“– Overview

**My Doctors** is a comprehensive **Flutter-based healthcare management platform** designed to centralize medical information and simplify access to healthcare services.

The application allows users to securely manage their medical records, prescriptions, reports, and medicine schedules while intelligently connecting them with **verified doctors, hospitals, and pharmacies** based on disease type and location.

The platform also empowers doctors, hospitals, and pharmacies to create professional profiles, ensuring transparency, trust, and efficient healthcare discovery.
